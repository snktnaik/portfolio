About

Portrait · 3:4

I'm Sanket — a designer who moves comfortably between research and pixels, shaping enterprise tools that people actually want to use.

My path runs from mechanical engineering in Goa, through a master's in Industrial Design at IIT Delhi, to product and UX design at TCS. Along the way I co-founded a 3D-printing startup and published research on accessible design.

That mix means I bring both the discipline of structured research and the instinct of someone who has shipped real products and run a business. I care about clarity, evidence, and details that hold up under scrutiny.
