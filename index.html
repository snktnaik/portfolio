01 / About
A designer shaped
by making things.

I come from mechanical engineering and industrial design — which means I'm equally comfortable thinking about manufacturing constraints and mapping user journeys. That breadth shapes how I approach every problem.

After an M.Des from IIT Delhi, where my thesis focused on safety-first design for elderly washroom environments, I've worked across agencies, a co-founded venture, and enterprise product teams. Today I design UX systems at TCS in Bengaluru.

Originally from Goa. Interested in football, guitar, travel, and cinema when not pushing pixels.
