About

A designer who moves between research and pixels — shaping enterprise tools that hold up under real scrutiny.

My path runs from mechanical engineering in Goa, through a master's in Industrial Design at IIT Delhi, to product and UX design at TCS. Along the way I co-founded a 3D-printing startup and published research on accessible design.

I bring the discipline of structured research and the instinct of someone who has shipped products and run a business — with a bias for clarity and evidence.
